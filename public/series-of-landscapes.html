Series of landscapes, 2011

Series of always the same landscape,
started from the same landscape.
To look at a landscape there are many ways, it is infinite.
A landscape changes over time,
it never stays the same, 'Panta rei'
what can happen?
Nobody knows.

Mixed media on paper, and wax, 29,5 X 20,5 cm
